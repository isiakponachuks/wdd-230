PROVIDING PERSPECTIVES ENTERPRISES is a wholly owned Nigerian Private Limited Liability Company established in 2021. It is an indigenous company to promote local content and direct investments.
PERSPECTIVES ENTERPRISES IS A DIVERSIFIED IMPORTATION & EXPORTATION, CONSTRUCTION PROCUREMENT, MAINTENANCE AND TANK CLEANING COMPANY IN THE OIL, GAS, POWER AND OTHER INDUSTRIES We have in our group a team of experienced engineers, managers, technicians and skilled workers drawn from the various disciplines in engineering and management. The company also maintains a working technical agreement with some companies overseas especially in Europe and America. These technical partners provide the much needed technical and equipment support on which our operation is anchored. With a very strong financial base predicated on sound financial management, Providing Perspectives Enterprises can mobilize promptly on any project within seven days of receipt of letter of contract award. It is this strong financial base together with our pool of highly technically qualified and experienced manpower that Providing Perspectives Enterprises executes contracts on schedule to the client’s or asset holder’s specification and satisfaction. .
